# Resume Notes

## What to emphasize
- simulated but realistic business environment
- workflow instead of chatbot
- approval gate / HITL for sensitive actions
- offline evals and trace-based debugging

## What not to claim
- do not claim real company ROI
- do not claim production deployment at enterprise scale
- do not claim direct refund execution if your project only proposes actions

## Safe phrasing
- "built in a simulated e-commerce support environment"
- "designed approval-based handling for sensitive support actions"
- "evaluated on a reproducible offline case set"
